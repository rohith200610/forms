To add a new expense:

  1. Navigate to the Expense List page
  2. Click the "Add Expense" button
  3. Fill in the expense details (title, amount, category, date)
  4. Click "Save" to add the expense to your list

You can also use the "Add Expense" button on the Dashboard for quick access.

To edit your monthly income:

  1. Go to the Monthly Salary page
  2. Click the "Edit" button in the top-right corner
  3. Modify the income amount in the Total Income card
  4. Click "Save" to confirm your changes

You can also update this information in your Profile settings.

  • Food & Dining: Restaurants, groceries, takeout
  • Transportation: Fuel, public transport, taxi, maintenance
  • Shopping: Clothing, electronics, household items
  • Entertainment: Movies, games, subscriptions
  • Bills & Utilities: Electricity, water, internet, phone
  • Healthcare: Medical expenses, insurance, pharmacy
  • Education: Courses, books, training
  • Travel: Vacation, business trips, accommodation

To export your expense data:

  1. Go to Settings from the Account menu
  2. Scroll down to the "Account Management" section
  3. Click "Export Data"
  4. Choose your preferred format (CSV, PDF, or Excel)
  5. Your data will be downloaded to your device

To customize your app appearance:

  1. Click on "Account" in the top navigation
  2. Select "Settings" from the dropdown
  3. In the "Appearance" section, choose your preferred theme
  4. Select your language from the dropdown
  5. Click "Save Changes" to apply
